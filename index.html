"Namaste! From Brp Mailo, an educator, IT professional, and web designer from Nepal, with a passion for both technology and literature."

Brp Mailo, an educator, IT professional, and web designer from Nepal, with a passion for both technology and literature. I hold a Diploma in Computer Hardware and Software, which has provided me with a solid foundation in computer systems, troubleshooting, and digital content creation. For the past three years, I have been working continuously in the IT department of Lovely Angels English Boarding School, where I currently serve as the Head of the IT Department. In this role, I manage the school's digital infrastructure, oversee system maintenance, and lead the integration of technology into the academic environment. I also have two years of experience as a graphic and layout designer at a printing press, where I designed a wide range of print materials including book covers, flyers, and educational resources. This enhanced my skills in visual design and layout techniques. Academically, I am pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Nepali Literature under the Faculty of Education at Tribhuvan University. My love for literature blends well with my technical skills, enabling me to express creativity in both digital and literary fields. As a web designer, I have developed and sold over 10 professional website templates. I also run an educational website, baburamphuyal.com.np, where I provide notes, teacher guides, curriculum content, and literary materials for students and educators. Additionally, I manage a YouTube channel named "Hamro Computer Guru", where I create tutorials on MS Excel, computer basics, internet tools, and various digital skills, all in the Nepali language — aiming to make tech education accessible to all.

My overall experience includes:

3+ years leading the IT department in a school setting
2 years in graphic/layout design at a printing press
Strong foundation in computer hardware and software
Proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and responsive web design
Creating educational and technical content for web and YouTube
